Relevant Coursework

BSc in Computer Science, International University, VNU-HCM, Vietnam

Mathematics

Discrete Mathematics

The branch that mostly have no relations to each other. Instead, this course provide a brief overview of how the classification looks like – depending on the research preferences. Such topics as mathematical logic, boolean algebra, graph theory are introduced at basic level.

Calculus

The most important subject that has been observed in every fields. Following by the understanding of discrete structures, students will learn the framework for modeling systems in accordance to changes.

Linear Algebra

The branch of mathematics focusing on vectors, vector spaces, matrices and linear transformations. Students will deal with linear equations, linear functions, matrix representation and determinants. This topic is widely recognized in the application of AI and Data Science.


Probability, Statistics, and Random Process

The study of numbers and data. Students are expected to know how to calculate the likelihood of an even happening – regarding the conditions and freedom, additionally how to collect – analyze – interpret data. Ultimately, students will learn the simple stochastic model – Markov Chain.

Computer Science

Object-Oriented Programming

The prototype of treating objects as class in programming. Its aims are to implement real-world entities - inheritance, hiding, polymorphism, etc. Students will use OOP principles to bind the data and functions together in order to operate seperatedly.

Data Science

Exploratory Data Analysis

The process used by data scientists to analyze – investigate and summarize the insights stripped from raw to semi-cleaned datasets, which is later on succeeded by procedures – preprocessing, modeling and deployment.

3. Computer Science

Web Application Development: The intervene of ML / DL models and interactive products. While most CS majors tend to align with web developers, Data Scientists and ML Engineers see this toolkit as a valuable decoration for their products. For the demand of competition requirements, back-end technologies like Django (Python), Node.js & Express.js help build the logical connection between dashboard and API.
